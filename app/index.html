What is Professional Amateur Photography?

Well that's kind of hard to explain. You know how you've always wanted to learn a new skill? There's that phase where you are okay at it but you don't feel like you can compete with the pros. Well stop stressing and make your amateur status a profession! That's what I did with photography. My work appears on movie posters, cassette tape covers, T-shirts and other stuff (all made by amateurs)! I mean just look at these darts!
